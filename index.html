About Me

Hi! I'm Nirali - part-time podiatrist, part-time budding front-end developer, full-time lactose intolerant. I started working as a podiatrist at the start of 2021 and started coding around March of 2022 after leaving one of my podiatry jobs. I've since learned the basics of HTML, CSS, JS, and React, and am currently completing the She Codes Plus course in Perth. When I'm not at work, you'll find me at a dance class, a wine bar, or a comedy club. Or, if you catch me at my best, in a quiet corner of my house, in a fluffy robe, 15 episodes deep into a TV show I started 8 hours ago.
